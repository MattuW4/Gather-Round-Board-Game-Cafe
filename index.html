About us

Gather Round is Leeds' premier board game cafe. When joining us you will find a friendly, welcoming space for seasoned board gamers or those wanting to explore something new. Our knowledgeable and accommodating team are always on hand to make game recommendations or to help trouble shoot anything, should you need it. As well as a wide selection of classic and new games to peruse, we serve a variety of drinks and food including local craft beers, teas and coffees, plus a seasonal food menu from our regional suppliers. You can jump straight to our booking page and reserve a table by hitting the button below to start your next board game adventure with us here at Gather Round!
